Real Estate Market Intelligence Report

Updated Weekly: Every Monday at 9:00 AM

Executive Summary

This comprehensive report analyzes the current real estate market landscape across key indicators including median home prices, inventory levels, delinquency trends, and housing supply dynamics.

The report is generated every Monday at 9:00 AM, synthesizing data from multiple authoritative sources to provide actionable market intelligence for real estate professionals and stakeholders.

Median Home Price Trends

Data Source: Redfin Market Tracker
URL: Redfin Public Data
Metrics: Week-over-week and year-over-year median home price comparisons

The Redfin dataset tracks median home prices across major markets, providing real-time insights into price trends and market movements. This data is crucial for understanding whether home values are appreciating or depreciating in key regions.

Supply vs Demand Indicators

Inventory Data: Realtor.com Research
URL: Realtor.com Research
Housing Supply Data: U.S. Census Bureau
API: Census API

Key metrics include:

  • Active Listings: Count of properties currently on the market
  • Inventory-to-Sales Ratio: Months of supply at current sales pace
  • Vacancy Rates: Percentage of unoccupied residential units
  • Construction Starts: Number of new residential construction projects initiated
  • Permits Issued: Building permits for new residential construction

Foreclosure & Delinquency Trends

Data Source: Consumer Financial Protection Bureau (CFPB)
URL: CFPB Delinquency Data
Metrics: Mortgage delinquency rates and foreclosure indicators

CFPB data tracks mortgage performance metrics including:

  • Delinquency rates (30, 60, 90+ days)
  • Loan modification activity
  • Foreclosure initiation trends
  • Loss mitigation activities

Mortgage Environment

The mortgage environment includes current lending standards, interest rate trends, and regulatory updates affecting the housing market. Key factors include:

  • Interest Rates: 30-year and 15-year mortgage rates
  • 50-Year Mortgages: Emerging products in the market
  • Lending Standards: Credit requirements and down payment requirements
  • Regulatory Changes: Updates to mortgage lending regulations and policies
  • Monetary Policy: Federal Reserve actions and economic outlook
Note: Mortgage policy data and lending environment updates are monitored through current market research and regulatory announcements.

Notable Regional & National Shifts

This section highlights significant market movements and notable changes in the real estate landscape:

  • National Price Trends: Overall median home price movements
  • Regional Variations: Price differences across major metropolitan areas
  • Supply/Demand Imbalances: Markets with tight inventory vs. oversupply
  • Lending Environment Changes: Shifts in mortgage availability and terms
  • Foreclosure Activity: Regional hot spots and trends
  • Construction Activity: New supply coming to market
